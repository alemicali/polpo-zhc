## The Italian Surety Market Opportunity

Italy represents one of the largest surety markets in Europe, driven primarily by mandatory bonding requirements in public procurement and real estate. Key product lines we intend to underwrite include:

1. **Public Procurement Bonds** (*Cauzioni per appalti pubblici*) — Governed by the recently reformed **D.Lgs 36/2023** (New Italian Public Contracts Code), including bid bonds, performance bonds, advance payment bonds, and warranty bonds. This segment alone generates over €1.5 billion in annual premiums in Italy.

2. **Real Estate Guarantees** (*Fideiussioni immobiliari*) — Mandatory under **D.Lgs 122/2005** to protect buyers of off-plan residential properties against developer insolvency. A growing and underserved niche with attractive risk profiles.

3. **Tax and Fiscal Bonds** (*Cauzioni fiscali*) — Required by the Italian Revenue Agency for VAT refunds, excise duties, and customs operations. Steady, recurring business with institutional counterparties.

4. **Customs Bonds** (*Cauzioni doganali*) — Guarantees for import/export duties and customs warehousing, supporting Italy's significant international trade flows.

5. **Commercial and Contractual Guarantees** — Including rent guarantees, supply contract bonds, and concession guarantees for private-sector counterparties.
