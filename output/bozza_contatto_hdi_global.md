Dear Sir/Madam,

### Introduction

I am writing on behalf of **Insurtech Solutions**, an Italian Managing General Agent (MGA) specializing exclusively in surety bonds and guarantees (*Ramo 15 — Cauzioni*) within the Italian market.

We are reaching out to HDI Global SE's Surety & Bonds Division to explore a strategic MGA partnership that would leverage our deep specialization in Italian surety to complement and enhance the excellent platform HDI has already established in Italy through **HDI Assicurazioni S.p.A.**

### About Insurtech Solutions

Insurtech Solutions is a newly established MGA registered with IVASS (*Registro Unico degli Intermediari — Section B*), purpose-built for the Italian surety market. Our founding team brings together seasoned professionals with direct experience in:

- **Italian surety underwriting** — deep knowledge of contractor risk assessment, financial analysis, and public procurement dynamics;
- **Regulatory expertise** — full command of the Italian legislative framework, including the new Public Procurement Code (*D.Lgs 36/2023*), Civil Code provisions on guarantees (*Art. 1936–1957 c.c.*), and real estate guarantees under *D.Lgs 122/2005*;
- **Technology-driven operations** — proprietary digital platform for policy issuance, portfolio monitoring, claims management, and real-time reporting to capacity providers;
- **Distribution network** — established relationships with specialized insurance brokers across Italy, covering key sectors and geographies.

### Why HDI Global SE — A Natural Fit

We have identified HDI Global SE as an ideal capacity partner for several compelling reasons:

1. **Existing Italian presence**: HDI Assicurazioni S.p.A. provides a strong, established brand and infrastructure in Italy. A surety MGA partnership would **extend HDI's Italian footprint into a high-margin specialty line** without requiring HDI Assicurazioni to build an internal surety team — a process that typically takes years and significant investment.

2. **Complementary positioning**: HDI Assicurazioni is well-known in Italy for property, liability, and motor lines. Adding a specialized surety capability through a dedicated MGA creates a **complete commercial offering** that brokers and corporate clients increasingly demand.

3. **Group synergies**: As part of the Talanx Group, HDI Global SE already has significant surety expertise at the group level. Our MGA model allows HDI to **deploy that expertise in Italy efficiently**, with local specialists managing origination, underwriting, and servicing.

4. **A+ financial strength**: HDI Global SE's S&P A+ rating is a critical differentiator in the Italian surety market, where beneficiaries — particularly public entities — increasingly scrutinize the financial standing of guarantors.

### Target Market & Product Focus

Our business plan targets the most attractive segments of the Italian surety market:

**Public Procurement Bonds** (*Cauzioni per Appalti Pubblici — D.Lgs 36/2023*)
- Bid bonds (*cauzione provvisoria*), performance bonds (*cauzione definitiva*), advance payment guarantees, warranty bonds
- Italy's public works pipeline exceeds €100 billion under the PNRR (National Recovery and Resilience Plan), driving sustained demand
- Target: qualified contractors (SOA-certified) with solid financial fundamentals

**Real Estate Guarantees** (*Fideiussioni Immobiliari — D.Lgs 122/2005*)
- Mandatory guarantees for off-plan property purchases — a legally required product with stable, recurring demand
- Lower severity profile with strong regulatory framework

**Commercial & Custom Bonds**
- Tax and customs guarantees, environmental bonds, contractual performance guarantees for private-sector clients
- Cross-selling opportunities with HDI Assicurazioni's existing corporate client base
