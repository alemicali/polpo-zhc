How It Works

Describe the goal.
Walk away.

Tell Polpo what you need in plain language. It plans the work, assigns agents, checks every result, and only pings you when it matters.

01

You describe

Tell Polpo what you need in natural language, via chat, Telegram, or API.

02

Polpo plans

AI generates a mission with tasks, dependencies, and agent assignments.

03

Agents execute

Crash-proof detached processes run each task with full tool access.

04

LLM judges review

Three independent reviewers score every result. Below threshold? Auto-fix.

05

You get results

Notified on completion. Escalated if it needs you. No babysitting.

Why Polpo

Finally, AI you can trust

Not another chatbot. A reliable system that plans, executes, reviews its own work, and asks before acting.

🔄

Self-correcting

LLM judges score every task. Below threshold? The agent gets specific feedback and fixes it — not a blind retry.

🛡️

Crash-proof

Agents run as detached processes. Kill Polpo, reboot the server, lose your connection — it picks up exactly where it left off.

🙋

Human-in-the-loop

Approval gates, checkpoints, and escalation chains. Your AI team handles the grunt work. You make the decisions.

📡

Talk from anywhere

Telegram, WhatsApp, Slack, email, or the built-in Web UI. Control your AI team from wherever you are.

🔧

30+ built-in tools

File I/O, shell, browser automation, email, image generation, web search, credential vault — agents have everything they need.

🏠

Runs anywhere

Your laptop, a $5 VPS, Docker, or your cloud. Your hardware, your API keys, your rules. Zero vendor lock-in.
